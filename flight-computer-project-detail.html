Description

I work on a rocket flight computer project with Imperial College Space Society colleagues. This flight computer will be placed and mounted in the rocket and will record telemetry from the rocket and it will transmit data from the rocket to the ground station. My responsibility involves developing the software for the computer and I also take care of the logistics of the project such as ordering components and requesting permits for the usage of lab equipment.
