ESA CanSat

Our team represented Czech Republic at internation finals of the ESA CanSat competition 2019. My primary role within the CanSat team was software. I wrote the software for the satellite and ground station in C++. I also developed a ground station data dashboard in JavaScript that visualized real-time data from the flight. Our project received Best Outreach Award and Award for the Final Report.
