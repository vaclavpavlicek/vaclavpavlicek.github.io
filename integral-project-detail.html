Description

During my 3-month summer internship at European Space Operations Centre in Darmstadt (Germany), I developed two Python tools - a mission plan checker and a transponder swap scheduler. The MPChecker is an automation tool used to validate the plan of the commands and their parameters before these are sent to the satellite. This ensures that the safety limits of the satellite are not violated. The transponder swap scheduler calculates the angles between the satellite antennas and the ground stations and suggests when to swap between transponders. The projects are in more detail described in a paper published in the Journal of Student Research.
