At the beginning of year 2, I participated in the NASA Space Apps Challenge together with other three colleagues from the Imperial College Space Society. Our project showed the importance of the light curves of asteroids in astronomy that help to estimate the size and shape of the asteroids. Our team was selected as one of the global nominees from other London teams.
