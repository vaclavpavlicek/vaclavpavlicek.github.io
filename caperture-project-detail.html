Description

We participated in the Imperial College Space Society hackathon during which we propose a docking system for two spacecrafts. After brainstorming about a couple of ideas we settled on Iris capturing mechanism. I used Fusion 360 to create an animation of the docking of two spacecrafts that used our system.
