Caperture

Together with friends we participated in the Imperial College Space Society hackathon during which we propose a docking system for two spacecrafts. After brainstorming about a couple of ideas we settled on Iris capturing mechanism. I used Fusion 360 to create animation of docking of two spacecrafts that used our system.
